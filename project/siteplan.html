ComicVault

This name reflects a universe of superheroes where fans can explore profiles, powers, and stories. It is short, memorable, and ideal for branding.

Site Purpose

The purpose of HeroVerse is to provide fans with a complete hub of Marvel and DC superheroes, including their origins, powers, biographies, and images. The site helps visitors discover new heroes and revisit their favorites with organized and visually engaging profiles

Scenarios

  • Which DC heroes have powers similar to Spider-Man?
  • What are the most powerful DC Comics heroes ranked?
  • What are the most powerful Marvel heroes ranked?

Color Schema

Primary: Purple (#6A0DAD) → Titles, buttons

Accent: Golden Yellow (#FFD700) → Highlights

Neutrals: Light Gray (#F5F5F5), Dark Gray (#333333) → Background & text

Typography

Font: Montserrat (used for all headings and body text)

Montserrat is clean, modern, and readable, fitting the comic theme while keeping the site professional and user-friendly.
